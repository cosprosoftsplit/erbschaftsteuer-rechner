Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Your premium features will be restored if you previously purchased them.
How do I remove ads?
You can unlock the premium version through an in-app purchase. This removes all advertisements permanently.
The app is not working correctly. What should I do?
Please try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you have the latest version installed. If the problem continues, contact us via email with a description of the issue.
How do I delete my data?
All your data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it by uninstalling the app. No data is stored on our servers.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers. In-app purchases are processed securely through Apple or Google.
